About Me

My name is Addyson Miller. I am a current first-year student at Fordham University on the pre-law track. I am an international studies major with a minor in political science and peace & justice studies. I am from Youngstown, Ohio where I have lived the past 19 years of my life. I am extremely passionate about human rights, social justice, advocacy, politics, fairness in the justice system, and equality for all.

In my free time I can be found doing creative activities such as painting, dancing, and writing. I am an avid reader of all genres including both fiction and nonfiction books. I am a committed music listener, and I am very passionate about music and the stories it can tell. I believe I am well-versed in current events and love to stay up to date with the news. I also enjoy spending time with my friends and family, exploring New York City with friends and by myself, and going to concerts.
